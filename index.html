Who we are

Great Bear Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (GBT) was established in August 2013 in Kathmandu, Nepal as Serofero Services (SFS). SFS changed its name and rebranded in mid-2017 to Great Bear Technologies.

GBT's mission is to empower mid to large sized organizations by helping them manage their operations and business processes with intuitive, intelligent, and cost-effective cloud-based and on-premise software solutions.
